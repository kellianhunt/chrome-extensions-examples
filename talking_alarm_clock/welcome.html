Welcome to The Talking Alarm!

The Talking Clock Extension allows you to create alarms with customizable sounds and turn off alarms with the voice command "OFF".

To enable voice commands, the alarm extension will need access to your microphone. If a popup requesting permission did not appear upon opening this page, check for a little camera symbol on the far right of your search bar. Click this symbol and allow the extension to use your microphone.

To test voice commands, create an alarm, and when it rings, say "Off" to your device.
